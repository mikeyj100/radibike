What is Radi Bike?

Radi Bike is a digital service that offers affordable transportation whilst being quick and easy to use.

Your radius!

This feature requires your location to be shared. This map shows the available Radi bikes within appropriate walking radius to you.

Pricing and membership

Login to our mobile app, walk to an available Radi Bike and press activate after scanning the QR code on the frame of the bike. Standard rate to use our bikes costs 10p per minute plus a £1 booking fee (pay-as-you-go). Frequient user? Buy our monthly pass for £30 giving you 300 minutes with Radi Bike.

Why use Radi Bike?

Healthy?

While using our service you achieve numerous health benefits such as improved heart health and improved muscle strength.

Anyone?

To use Radi Bike you are required to have a provisional lisence and to know how to ride a bike.

Leisure?

Perhaps you are site seeing, Radi Bike is an excellent way to venture around at your own pace.

Eco-friendly?

Riding a Radi Bike is the cleanest method of transportation, resulting in less polution, traffic and congestion on the roads.

Efficient?

Providing there is Radi Bike's available within appropriate walking radius, getting around busy cities couldn't get any easier.

Wellbeing?

Cycling is a well known to improve your mental health, as it helps you sleep better and improves your mood.
